wp-elements-de45bfd459d6c6596d67d34e938d02ae\" id=\"facts\"><span class=\"ez-toc-section\" id=\"Interesting_Facts_about_the_Pyramids_of_Egypt\"></span>Interesting Facts about the Pyramids of Egypt<span class=\"ez-toc-section-end\"></span></h2>\n<p>The Pyramids of Giza were built almost 5,000 years ago, at the beginning of human recorded history.</p>\n<p>The Great Pyramid of Giza, also known as the Pyramid of Khufu or the Pyramid of Cheops, is the oldest of the three main pyramids on the Giza plateau. This pyramid was built around 2570 BC and it is the oldest of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World. In fact, it is the only Ancient Wonder to remain largely intact.</p>\n<p>For 3,800 years, the Great Pyramid was the tallest building in the world, until the Lincoln Cathedral was built in 1311 AD in England.</p>\n<p>When people refer to the “Pyramids of Giza,” typically they are referring to the three large pyramids that are located in the Giza complex. These three pyramids include the Great Pyramid and its two neighbors, the Pyramid of Khafre and the Pyramid of Menkaure.</p>\n<p>The pyramids were built as tombs for the pharaohs of Egypt. Inside of the pyramid is the “King’s Chamber,” the location of the sarcophagus.</p>\n<p>The Pyramids of Giza are located on the Giza Plateau, which is also referred to as the Giza Necropolis or the Giza pyramid complex. Nine pyramids, the Great Sphinx, and a collection of smaller tombs sit on this plateau.</p>\n<p>The Giza Plateau bumps right up against the sprawling city of Giza. If you like the idea of booking a hotel room with a unique view, there are numerous hotels where you can have a room with a view of the Sphinx and/or the Great Pyramid.</p>\n<figure class=\"wp-block-image\"><img alt=\"Camel Ride\" decoding=\"async\" src=\"https://www.earthtrekkers.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Camel-Ride.jpg.optimal.jpg\"/></figure>\n</div>\n<div class=\"wp-block-group has-global-padding is-layout-constrained wp-block-group-is-layout-constrained\">\n<h2 class=\"wp-block-heading has-text-align-center has-main-color has-text-color has-link-color wp-elements-143297f5f23bceba1ed15cb17815a681\" id=\"todo\"><span class=\"ez-toc-section\" id=\"Best_Things_to_Do_at_the_Pyramids_of_Giza\"></span>Best Things to Do at the Pyramids of Giza<span class=\"ez-toc-section-end\"></span></h2>\n<ul class=\"wp-block-list\">\n<li>See the pyramids of Khufu, Khafre, and Menkaure</li>\n<li>Go inside one of the pyramids</li>\n<li>Visit Panoramic Point</li>\n<li>Take a selfie with the Sphinx</li>\n<li>Visit the Valley Temple</li>\n<li>Watch the Sound and Light Show</li>\n</ul>\n<figure class=\"wp-block-image\"><img alt=\"Map of the Pyramids of Giza\" decoding=\"async\" src=\"https://www.earthtrekkers.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Map-of-the-Pyramids-of-Giza.jpg.optimal.jpg\"/></figure>\n<p class=\"has-text-align-center\"><em>Map of the Giza Plateau</em></p>\n</div>\n<div class=\"wp-block-group has-global-padding is-layout-constrained wp-block-group-is-layout-constrained\">\n<h3 class=\"wp-block-heading has-text-align-center\" id=\"h-the-pyramids-of-khufu-khafre-and-menkaure\"><span class=\"ez-toc-section\" id=\"The_Pyramids_of_Khufu_Khafre_and_Menkaure\"></span>The Pyramids of Khufu, Khafre, and Menkaure<span class=\"ez-toc-section-end\"></span></h3>\n<p>Without a doubt, the best thing to do while on the Giza Plateau is to see the three massive pyramids that dominate the landscape.</p>\n<p>To fully appreciate their enormity, get up close to the Great Pyramid. The limestone blocks are massive. Just imagine the manpower it took to build something like this. It’s even more amazing to think that people built these pyramids almost 5,000 years ago.</p>\n<h3 class=\"wp-block-heading has-text-align-center\" id=\"h-go-inside-one-of-the-pyramids\"><span class=\"ez-toc-section\" id=\"Go_Inside_One_of_the_Pyramids\"></span>Go Inside One of the Pyramids<span class=\"ez-toc-section-end\"></span></h3>\n<p>For an additional fee, you can go inside the Great Pyramid of Giza, the Pyramid of Khafre, and the Pyramid of Menkaure. More details later in this post <a href=\"#inside\">(or skip ahead now).</a></p>\n<h3 class=\"wp-block-heading has-text-align-center\" id=\"h-panoramic-point\"><span class=\"ez-toc-section\" id=\"Panoramic_Point\"></span>Panoramic Point<span class=\"ez-toc-section-end\"></span></h3>\n<p>This is one of the best viewpoints of the pyramids of Giza. From this spot, you can see six of the nine pyramids that sit on the Giza Plateau.</p>\n<figure class=\"wp-block-image\"><img alt=\"Panoramic Point Egypt Pyramids\" decoding=\"async\" src=\"https://www.earthtrekkers.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Panoramic-Point-Egypt-Pyramids.jpg.optimal.jpg\"/></figure>\n</div>\n<div class=\"wp-block-group has-global-padding is-layout-constrained wp-block-group-is-layout-constrained\">\n<h3 class=\"wp-block-heading has-text-align-center\" id=\"h-talk-a-selfie-with-the-sphinx\"><span class=\"ez-toc-section\" id=\"Talk_a_Selfie_with_the_Sphinx\"></span>Talk a Selfie with the Sphinx<span class=\"ez-toc-section-end\"></span></h3>\n<p>For a close-up view of the Sphinx with the Pyramids in the background, make sure you visit the viewing platform that sits next to the Sphinx. If you want a photo “kissing the Sphinx,” this is the place to do it.</p>\n<figure class=\"wp-block-image\"><img alt=\"Great Sphinx\" decoding=\"async\" src=\"https://www.earthtrekkers.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Great-Sphinx.jpg.optimal.jpg\"/></figure>\n<h3 class=\"wp-block-heading has-text-align-center\" id=\"h-go-on-a-camel-ride\"><span class=\"ez-toc-section\" id=\"Go_on_a_Camel_Ride\"></span>Go on a Camel Ride<span class=\"ez-toc-section-end\"></span></h3>\n<p>There have been reports about animal abuse, not just to camels, but horses too, at the pyramids. In some cases, the animals are sometimes beaten and overworked during the day, and offered little chance to take a break.</p>\n<p>We didn’t witness any of this ourselves. The camels we saw looked in good health and seemed to be getting plenty of rest. But you never know what goes on behind the scenes and the circumstances may be different when you visit (a busier time of day, a different person attending the animals, etc.). Therefore, if you are considering this experience, it is important to educate yourself on this subject first.</p>\n<p>For more information about how to have an ethical camel riding experience, read this article on <a href=\"https://matadornetwork.com/read/ethical-camel-riding/\" rel=\"noreferrer noopener\" target=\"_blank\">Matador Network.</a> To read more about the treatment of horses and camels in Egypt, read this article on the <a href=\"https://www.nytimes.com/2019/04/13/world/middleeast/egypt-pyramids-animal-abuse.html\" rel=\"noreferrer noopener\" target=\"_blank\">New York Times. </a></p>\n<h3 class=\"wp-block-heading has-text-align-center\" id=\"h-visit-the-valley-temple\"><span class=\"ez-toc-section\" id=\"Visit_the_Valley_Temple\"></span>Visit the Valley Temple<span class=\"ez-toc-section-end\"></span></h3>\n<p>Located right next to the Sphinx is the Valley Temple. This temple was used in the mummification process of King Khafre.</p>\n<h3 class=\"wp-block-heading has-text-align-center\" id=\"h-watch-the-sound-and-light-show\"><span class=\"ez-toc-section\" id=\"Watch_the_Sound_and_Light_Show\"></span>Watch the Sound and Light Show<span class=\"ez-toc-section-end\"></span></h3>\n<p>Each night, the Sphinx and the pyramids are illuminated in a rainbow of colors. For one hour, the Sphinx narrates the history of the pyramids.</p>\n<p>The Sound and Light Show gets mixed reviews. Some people love it, some say it’s boring, almost everyone says that it is outdated (it started in the 1960’s and hasn’t changed much since then).</p>\n<p>Tickets start at $20 USD and can be reserved in advance. <a href=\"https://www.soundandlight.show/en\" rel=\"noreferrer noopener\" target=\"_blank\">Click here</a> to learn more. To get up to the King’s Chamber, you will walk up a ramp through the Grand Gallery until you reach the King’s Chamber. In this dark, hot room is the empty granite sarcophagus.</p>\n<figure class=\"wp-block-image\"><img alt=\"Entrance into Great Pyramid\" decoding=\"async\" src=\"https://www.earthtrekkers.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Entrance-into-Great-Pyramid.jpg.optimal.jpg\"/></figure>\n<p class=\"has-text-align-center\"><em>In this photo you can see the entrance into the Great Pyramid.</em></p>\n</div>\n<div class=\"wp-block-group has-global-padding is-layout-constrained wp-block-group-is-layout-constrained\">\n<div aria-hidden=\"true\" class=\"wp-block-spacer\" style=\"height:30px\"></div>\n<figure class=\"wp-block-image aligncenter is-resized\"><img alt=\"Grand Gallery Great Pyramid\" decoding=\"async\" src=\"https://www.earthtrekkers.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Grand-Gallery-Great-Pyramid.jpg.optimal.jpg\" style=\"width:600px\"/></figure>\n<p class=\"has-text-align-center\"><em>The Grand Gallery</em></p>\n<p class=\"has-background-6-background-color has-background\" style=\"padding-top:var(--wp--preset--spacing--small);padding-right:var(--wp--preset--spacing--medium);padding-bottom:var(--wp--preset--spacing--small);padding-left:var(--wp--preset--spacing--medium)\"><strong>PRO TRAVEL TIP:</strong> You can take photos inside of the Great Pyramid with a cellphone, at no additional fee. It’s a much different experience than visiting the colorful tombs in the <a href=\"https://www.earthtrekkers.com/how-to-visit-valley-of-the-kings-luxor-egypt/\" target=\"_blank\">Valley of the Kings</a> or climbing down the tunnels of the <a href=\"https://www.earthtrekkers.com/dahshur-memphis-and-saqqara-day-trip-from-cairo-giza/\" target=\"_blank\">Bent Pyramid and the Red Pyramid</a> (this feels like an adventure). But it is still a thrill to stand inside of this amazing Ancient Wonder of the World.</p>\n<p>If you are not sure you want to spend the time and money to go inside the Great Pyramid, don’t feel bad about skipping it. December and January, it is common for the pyramids to be hazy in the morning. But you can’t count on this happening.</p>\n<p>Because of the possibility of hazy skies in the morning, the best time to visit the pyramids in the winter months is midday and in the afternoon. If you prefer to go in the morning, when the pyramids are less crowded, you will have to keep your fingers crossed for clear skies.</p>\n<p>If you will be visiting Egypt between February and November, that morning haze is unlikely.</p>\n<p>Between March and October, expect hot temperatures.
